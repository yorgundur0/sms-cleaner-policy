Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the SMS Cleaner+ iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines how our application interacts with your data and what measures we take to protect it.

Data Collection and Usage

SMS Cleaner+ uses Apple's Message Filter Extension technology to identify and filter unwanted SMS and MMS messages. Here's how we handle your data:

SMS Content: SMS Cleaner+ does not collect, store, or share your SMS messages with any third parties. All message filtering happens directly on your device using Apple's secure Message Filter Extension framework.

Message Filter Extension: The app leverages Apple's built-in Message Filter Extension technology, which is designed with privacy in mind. This technology allows our app to analyze messages for filtering purposes without compromising your privacy or storing message content.

Analytics: We use Firebase to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve the app's performance and user experience. The data collected through Firebase includes:

  • App usage statistics (e.g., how often features are used)
  • Crash reports
  • Basic device information (device model, iOS version)

Firebase analytics does not collect or store any of your SMS message content. The analytics data is used solely for improving app functionality and user experience.

Security

SMS Cleaner+ is designed with your privacy as a priority:

  • SMS messages are never stored or transmitted outside of your device
  • The message filtering process happens locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your personal messages
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any anonymous data that is collected

User Control

You have full control over the SMS filtering functionality:

You can enable or disable the SMS filtering feature at any time through your iOS device settings:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Select Messages
  3. Tap Unknown & Spam
  4. Toggle SMS Cleaner+ on or off

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
